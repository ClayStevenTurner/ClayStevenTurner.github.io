About Me
I live and work in Orange County, California where I use data and creative problem solving to map the desires of customers to product development roadmaps and go-to-market strategy so that incremental value is delivered on a sustainable cadence, maximizing institutional trust and customer loyalty.

“The key is not to prioritize what's on your schedule, but to schedule your priorities”

- Stephen Covey


As a product owner, here are my fundamental areas of accountability:

I Know Customers

Whether new, old, prospective, or recently churned, I engage customers through surveys, media, and direct contact to articulate their needs.

I Define Success

I use data to identify and track performance indicators relative to business goals, market forces, and emerging opportunities.

I Mediate Production

Stand up, call in, or round table, I pull together cross-functional teams to do their best work together using Scrum and Agile methodologies.
